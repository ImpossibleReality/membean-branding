Brand Guidelines

Hello! We are very proud of our brand, and care alot about how it is presented in the world. We've created some guidelines to help you use our brand and assets to ensure consistency across our design assets.

Usage

The green wordmark and logo are our primary marks and should be used whenever possible. The green marks should ONLY be used on a white background. If you are using the marks on any other background color, use either the black or white monochrome marks.

Do not place the colored Membean logo over background colors that clash. If in doubt, use monochrome!

The wordmark should have a margin of clear space on all sides around it, equal to half of the wordmark height.

The logo should have a margin of clear space equal to one-third of the logo height on the top and bottom, and equal to half the logo height on the left and right.

We have established a minimum size to ensure that the legibility of our marks isn’t compromised and they are always easy to read. The minimum size of our wordmark should be a height of 21px on screen, or 0.25 in (6.35mm) in print. The minimum size of our logo should be a height of 42px on screen, or 0.5 in (12.7mm) in print.

We politely ask that you don't:

  • Use any logos or similar imagery to represent Membean other than what is found in the Membean Brand Assets
  • Rotate the Membean logo or wordmark
  • Change the colors on the logo or wordmark
  • Overprint or obstruct any part of the logo or wordmark
  • Add special effects to the logo or wordmark
  • Use old versions or any other marks or logos to represent our brand

Typography

Our typeface is a visual representation of Membean’s tone and voice. It’s a friendly, whimsical and straightforward typeface which complements our digital and print experiences. Our official typeface is the lovely Brandon Text, developed by Hannes Von Döhren in 2012.

Titles & Headings

Designs will generally have a single title, and potentially many section headings. Please use Brandon Text Medium for all titles and headings, and the following sizes:

  • H1: 48px
  • H2: 30px
  • H3: 21px
  • H4: 18px
  • H5: 17px
  • H6: 16px

Subtitles

Subtitles are generally used to display an introduction to a web page or content section.

  • Font Face: Brandon Text Regular
  • Font Size: 24px

Regular Text

In general, we prefer that copy which conveys important content be displayed on a white background.

  • Font Face: Brandon Text Regular
  • Font Color: #20292D
  • Font Size: 18px

Accessibility

We strive to meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA accessibility standards in all of our designs. Please be sure that you are familiar with these standards, and that your designs are compliant with these standards – particularly the color contrast requirements.
